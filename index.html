Curt Wells is an artist based in Enumclaw, WA. He has had considerable experience in various areas of the arts, graphic design, and printmaking. His focus consists of Linoleum Block Printing -- the art of carving from blocks to then create a stamp. The blocks are then rolled with ink, and printed onto paper multiple times. Some prints have multiple colors which require either multiple blocks for different inks, or a reduction print, where the block is continuously carved after successive prints.
